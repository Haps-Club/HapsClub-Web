About Haps Club

A daily guide to LA
that respects your time.

Haps Club is a short, curated homepage of what's actually worth doing in Los Angeles right now — updated daily, with a weekly email on Fridays. Written by someone who actually goes.

What you get

The website is updated every day — fresh picks for what's on tonight, what to plan for the weekend, and a few things worth blocking off your calendar further out. Land here midweek and you'll find your night.

Once a week, on Friday morning, the best of the week's picks land in your inbox as a single short email. No top-ten lists, no SEO bait, no "37 things to do this weekend in LA." Just a handful I've vetted, with the address, the time, and a direct link to the venue.

Cards link straight to the source — no aggregators, no review sites — so you're one tap away from booking, not three.

How I pick

I live in LA. I go to the things. The bar I'm sending you to is a bar I've sat at; the patio I'm telling you to book is one I've eaten on. Where a venue is too new for me to have visited, I'll say so plainly and tell you what's known.

I don't take payment for placement. The only sponsored content is clearly labeled "Sponsored," appears at the bottom of the email, and never crosses into the picks. If you're a venue or event hoping to be featured, the only way in is to be good — and the way to tell me is below.

What this isn't

This isn't a comprehensive listings site — Time Out exists, the LA Times exists, you have Google. This is the opposite: a small, opinionated edit. If today's homepage has six things on it, it's because six things are worth your night.

It's also not a social club, a paid membership, or anything that requires you to buy a ticket. It's a free email and a free site. The "Become a member" framing on the homepage is just our way of saying: sign up, you're in.
