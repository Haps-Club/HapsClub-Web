About Haps Club

A daily guide to LA
that respects your time.

Haps Club is a short, curated homepage of what's actually worth doing in Los Angeles right now — updated daily, with a weekly email digest. Written by someone who actually goes.

What you get

One short list, every day.

The site is updated every day — fresh picks for what's on tonight, what to plan for the weekend, and a few things worth blocking off your calendar further out. Land here any day of the week and you'll find your night.

Once a week, the best of the picks land in your inbox as a single short email. No top-ten lists, no SEO bait, no "37 things to do this weekend in LA." Just a handful I've vetted, with the address, the time, and a direct link to the venue.

How I pick

I live here. I go to the things.

The bar I'm sending you to is one I've sat at; the patio I'm telling you to book is one I've eaten on. Where a venue is too new for me to have visited, I'll say so plainly and tell you what's known.

I don't take payment for placement. The only sponsored content is clearly labeled "Sponsored," appears at the bottom, and never crosses into the picks. The way to be featured is to be good — the way to tell me is below.

What this isn't

Not a listings site. Not paid.

This isn't a comprehensive listings site — Time Out exists, the LA Times exists, you have Google. This is the opposite: a small, opinionated edit. If today's homepage has six things on it, it's because six things are worth your night.

It's also not a social club, a paid membership, or anything that requires a ticket. It's a free email and a free site. "Become a member" is just our way of saying: sign up, you're in.

Haps Rec

Some picks come from a small group of LA-based friends who share what they're going to. When you see a card with a coral Haps Rec tag, it's coming from one of them. Want in? Use the form below and say so — we add a few new members each month.
