About the evening

AN EVENING IN THE GARDEN

Twentieth Street Garden and Haps Club come together for a summer evening in one of West Adams’ most quietly magnetic spaces — a lush open-air backyard in full June bloom.

Your ticket includes a beverage at the door and live entertainment, with BBQ smoked and slow-roasted on site — pulled pork and a pulled-mushroom option for the vegetarians.

Good people, great food, live music — come as you are.

Twentieth Street Garden is a backyard gathering space in Los Angeles. They host seasonal dinners, intimate concerts, and neighborhood socials — a welcoming spot to share good food, music, and community in West Adams.
