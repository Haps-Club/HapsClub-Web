About the evening

The garden is in full summer bloom

Twentieth Street Garden and Haps Club are back together for a Summer Garden Party in West Adams — set in the same lush open-air backyard you loved in May, now dressed for June.

Your ticket includes a welcome drink and a plate of three tacos from Tacos Como En El D.F., the beloved neighborhood truck. Craft cocktails and garden-fresh mocktails will be flowing all night.

Come for the neighbors, the new faces, the music, and the kind of easy summer evening that only happens when the setting is just right.
