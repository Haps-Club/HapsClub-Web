About the evening

An evening in bloom,
in West Adams

Twentieth Street Garden and Haps Club return this summer for an evening in one of West Adams' most quietly beautiful spaces — a lush open-air backyard in full June bloom.

Your ticket includes a welcome drink of your choice and a plate of three tacos from Tacos Como En El D.F., the beloved neighborhood taco truck. Craft cocktails and garden-fresh mocktails will be made throughout the evening from ingredients grown on site.

Expect good conversation, new faces, and the kind of unhurried summer evening that feels genuinely special.
