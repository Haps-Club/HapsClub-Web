About the evening

AN EVENING

in the garden

Twentieth Street Garden and Haps Club are throwing a summer party in one of West Adams' most quietly magnetic spaces — a lush open-air backyard in full June bloom.

Your ticket includes a welcome drink and three tacos from Tacos Como En El D.F., the beloved neighborhood truck. Craft cocktails and garden-fresh mocktails all night, made with ingredients from the garden itself.

Come for the people, stay for the vibe. This one always feels different.
