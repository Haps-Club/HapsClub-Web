The Destination

Where the day just begins.

Above the canyon. Past the gate. Through the courtyard and up the limestone steps. The Macallan Villa is not where the afternoon ends — it is where the night actually begins.

Every estate visited points here. Every pour at every door, every drive between hilltops builds to one address — invite-only, never open to the public, and quiet for the rest of the year.

The Day

From hilltop
to the Villa.

A curated arc, not a schedule. The afternoon moves between Bel-Air's most architecturally significant active listings — each one paired to the next Macallan pour — and lands at the Villa for the evening proper.

2:00 PM
Arrival
Bel-Air Open Houses begin.
Private walkthroughs of Compass's finest active listings — architectural landmarks, hilltop estates, gated properties. Hosted by the top-producing agents who represent them. Two hours, on foot and by car, through the neighborhoods that built modern Los Angeles.
Throughout
The Tour
A pour at every estate.
At each stop, a curated tasting. The Macallan's range introduced in sequence — each expression paired to the character of the home it's poured in. Sherry Oak in the library. Double Cask on the loggia. Edition No. X in the kitchen of a Lloyd Wright restoration.
4:00 PM
Cocktail Hour
Arrival at The Macallan Villa.
Through the courtyard. Welcome pour at the bar. The signature Old Fashioned program opens, the Lounge opens, and the collection wall reveals. One hour of cocktails and conversation before the table is set.
5:00 PM
Dinner
A table by Chef Chris Sayegh.
A multi-course menu — plant-forward, sustainability-led, paired course by course to the pours. Designed with The Macallan's culinary program and built specifically for the Villa. Ninety minutes at the table.
6:30 PM
The Finale
A party until the night ends.
The deck opens. The Old Fashioned program shifts into a late-night cocktail bar. The conversations that started in Bel-Air keep going — under the Valley lights, on the room that built it. No last call. The night ends when it ends.
