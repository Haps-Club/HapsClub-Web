Save the Dates

The final seats at Saturday's table, and the August nights to lock in now.

Hi, we are down to the final seats at our first Haps Club long table this Saturday, so this issue leads with it and then looks ahead to a strong stretch of August, a beloved village coming home, a festival on the sand, and a free food-and-music night in Culver City.

If you want first word on the next dinner, or a plus one for anything below, come hang in our WhatsApp group. Now, this week and the rest of summer.

The lead pick · This Saturday

Haps Club x The Long Table

One communal table, one vegetarian menu, an evening that runs late.

Sat Jul 25 · 6–9 PM · private residence near Hyde Park, South LA · ticketed

A curated group around a single long table, with candlelight, music and a menu made for the night. Drinks and hors d'oeuvres to open, a seated vegetarian dinner, then dessert, hosted by Michael, Anjali and Svetlana. This is our first, and there will be more, so if Saturday is not yours, join the group for the next one. Otherwise, reserve on Luma.

The comeback · Saturday, Aug 15

Palisades Village comes back

The neighborhood's living room, open again after the fire.

Sat Aug 15 · grand reopening · 15225 Palisades Village Lane, Pacific Palisades · free

Nineteen months after the 2025 fire, Caruso's open-air village returns with its shops, restaurants and the Bay Theater, and a full day of festivities to mark the reopening. A quiet, hopeful thing for the Westside. See the reopening.

On the calendar · Aug 22

Jazz on the Beach at Dockweiler

A festival lineup, feet in the sand, the ocean at your back.

Sat Aug 22 · doors 11 AM · Dockweiler Beach, 12000 Vista Del Mar, Playa del Rey · ticketed

The LA Jazz Festival closes summer on the sand with Janelle Monae, Charlie Wilson, Parliament Funkadelic feat. George Clinton, Big Freedia, Nubya Garcia, Poncho Sanchez and more. Grab the Saturday or Sunday pass.

On the calendar · Fri, Aug 14

KCRW Summer Nights at the Wende

A Good Food night market, DJs, and a Cold War museum after dark.

Fri Aug 14 · 6–10 PM · The Wende Museum, 10808 Culver Blvd, Culver City · free, RSVP required

KCRW turns the Wende into a dance floor for the Good Food Night Market edition, with a curated lineup of bites, DJ sets from Anne Litt and Nassir Nassirzadeh, and late access to the galleries. Free and all ages. RSVP on Humanitix.

AWOL · Always West of Lincoln · Venice

The Tasting Kitchen returns

A Venice landmark, back with the chef who made it.

Now open · 1633 Abbot Kinney Blvd, Venice · dinner

One of Abbot Kinney's defining restaurants is open again, with chef Casey Lane back at the pass and the Mediterranean-leaning cooking that made its name. A worthy first stop on any Venice night out. Book a table.

AWOL · Marina del Rey

Get on the water in the Marina

Paddle the flat water, or ride the dollar boat around the harbor.

All summer · Marina del Rey · kayak and SUP rentals, or the WaterBus for $1

The largest man-made small-craft harbor in the country is calm, warm and made for a morning kayak or stand-up paddle. Not feeling the arms, the summer WaterBus hops you around the harbor for a dollar through Labor Day. See what is on the water.

AWOL · Venice

First Fridays on Abbot Kinney

The boulevard's monthly food-truck street party.

Fri Aug 7 · 5–9:30 PM · Abbot Kinney Blvd, Venice · free

First Friday of the month, the boulevard turns into LA's premier food-truck night, with shops open late and music in the street. Come hungry and wander. See the details.

That's the week. One last summer table on Saturday, and a calendar worth filling for August. If you make it to one of these, tell me what you thought. — Michael
