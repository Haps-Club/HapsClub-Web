Red, White & the Long Weekend

Our dinner on Thursday, the best of the Fourth, and a few west-of-Lincoln ideas.

A day early this week, because there's too much packed in to wait until Tuesday. Thursday we're hosting a long-table dinner at Hermanito; Saturday the city lights up, from the free Grand Park block party to the Bowl's fireworks; and we've got a few west-of-Lincoln ideas for staying coastal.

The lead pick · This Thursday

Our Hermanito Broadway dinner

One long table, one set menu, the kind of dinner that runs late.

Thu Jul 2 · 6–9 PM · Hermanito, 802 Broadway, Santa Monica · ticketed

We're taking over Hermanito on Broadway for an evening of family-style plates and good company — meet and mingle over a drink, a shared dinner of tacos and small plates, then dessert. The next in our Broadway Dinner Series, and seats are limited. Reserve your seat on Luma.

The Fourth · Free · Downtown

Grand Park's Summer Block Party

The free downtown blowout, capped by 700 drones.

Sat Jul 4 · 4–9:30 PM · Gloria Molina Grand Park, 200 N Grand Ave, DTLA · free, all ages

Billed as the West Coast's largest free Fourth of July celebration: two stages of live music (Tatiana Tate, DJ Anthony Valadez), interactive art, and a 700-drone show over DTLA to close the night. Free, all ages, pet-friendly. Take the Metro B or D line to Civic Center. Full lineup and RSVP.

The Fourth · Hollywood

The Bowl's Fireworks Spectacular

Orchestra under the stars, fireworks over the shell.

Thu Jul 2 – Sat Jul 4 · Hollywood Bowl · ticketed

The classic — three nights of fireworks with a live orchestra and a guest headliner. Pack a picnic box and take the park-and-ride shuttle to skip the stacked parking. Check this year's lineup and seats.

♥ Haps pick · From the Haps WhatsApp

Venice Tribe DJs July 4th Beach Bash

Twelve years deep, feet in the sand, fireworks at nine.

Sat Jul 4 · 1–10 PM · Marina Peninsula beach (near 1 Eastwind, MdR) · $5 suggested

Straight from the Haps WhatsApp, where Stefanie put us onto her July 4th go-to: a twelfth-annual community beach party from the Venice Tribe DJs and friends. Music in the sand from midday through the Marina del Rey fireworks at 9 PM, family-friendly, five dollars suggested. Bring a blanket and snacks to share, rideshare in, and look for the purple gorilla. RSVP on Partiful.

The Fourth · Westside · Free

Fireworks over Marina del Rey

The same show, with the harbor and a drink.

Sat Jul 4 · fireworks 9–9:20 PM · Burton Chace Park, Marina del Rey · free

If a beach dance party isn't your speed, the quieter play on the same harbor is the city's 20-minute fireworks show over the Main Channel, best taken in from Burton Chace Park or Fisherman's Village. Come early for a spot on the grass. Plan your spot.

AWOL · Always West of Lincoln · Santa Monica

The Annenberg Community Beach House

Marion Davies' old estate, now open to everyone.

Open for the summer · 415 Pacific Coast Hwy, Santa Monica · free to wander, pool pass extra

Marion Davies' Gold Coast estate is now a public beach club, with the historic pool open for the season, a splash pad, beach volleyball, and the Back on the Beach Café on the sand. Grounds are free; the pool is a small pass. They also run a Sunset Swim on Friday, July 10, 6–9 PM.

AWOL · Venice

Work it off at the Venice courts

Paddle tennis, pickleball, and volleyball on the sand.

Any day · Venice Beach Recreation Center, Ocean Front Walk · free

If the long weekend needs burning off, the oceanfront Venice Beach courts are the move: historic paddle tennis, pickup beach volleyball, and pickleball nets that keep multiplying. Not a racket person? The Strand runs flat and fast for a morning run, bike, or skate.

AWOL · Venice

Dinner at Gjelina

The Abbot Kinney standard-bearer, still worth it.

Any night · 1429 Abbot Kinney Blvd, Venice · dinner daily 5–10:30 PM

If you're staying west and want one reservation worth keeping, this is still it — wood-fired vegetables, blistered pizzas, and that candlelit back patio. Book a table.

However you spend it, do it well. Come find us at Hermanito on Thursday. — Michael & the Haps Club community
